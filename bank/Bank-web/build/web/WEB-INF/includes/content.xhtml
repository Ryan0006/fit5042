Public member

A member of public can search for a banking transaction by its type, banking transaction name, banking transaction No, after which search results are displayed. They also have the option to view the full details of a banking transaction from the search result. In addition, they can perform banking transactions using the system, e.g transferring funds between accounts as well as deposit and withdraw money etc.

Bank worker

The system permits bank workers to do everything a member of public can plus the following features: Bank workers can view/add/update/delete users (either public or banking workers). They also have the access to search members of public by a combination of ID, last name, first name, type and email, then view the personal details (including the information about any previous and existing used banking transactions(s)) of any particular member of public selected. In addition, bank workers can add and delete banking transaction types.
